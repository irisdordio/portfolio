Catalogue design for the exhibition Novas Novas Cartas Portuguesas, curated by Tobi Maier at Galeria Quadrum, Lisbon — embodied on the event of Novas Cartas Portuguesas' 50th anniversary. Many thanks to Tobi Maier for inviting me to design the exhibition's publication and providing the opportunity to share one of my texts, "On The Fetish For Nuns", as part of the publication.
