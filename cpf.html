We seek to deconstruct and subvert contemporary stereotypes of happiness, creating a manifesto that multiplies itself on various supports aiming to make the wickedness in this discourse explicit – masking the appeal of productivity and consumption while promoting individual well-being. This concept of dystopian and capitalist nature seeks to unite the collective and the individual, neutralizing the possibility of critical thinking about well-being in social and political terms. Collaboration w/ → Maria Beauvalet and Victoria Pristoica.
